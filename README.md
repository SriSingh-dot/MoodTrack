# MoodTrack
- This website is for mood tracking. I actually made it for my best friend who is not well mentally but she do love tracking her day and mood. 

- But i bet many girlies love to journal their day too.

# Working on Site
- On the home page click "Enter" button displayed under the heading.
- You will be displayed another page which is actually the index of the whole project.
- Click enter to one of the options from the three.
- On clicking the enter on the break taking page, you will have to scroll down do review some photos with a sentence under it after you are done cick back and you are back to index.
- In the second option of the index, you will have a moodtracker,
- you can click on the mood you are in and the date clocks will be colored in the specific color assigned to the mood.
- In third option you can record an event or anything for a specific date to store and remember.
